How to view Students profile with an Faculty ID

Step 1: Log in to the student information system (SIS).

The first step is to log in to the SIS. This is usually done through a web browser. The login credentials will be provided by the institution.

Processing..

Step 2: Enter the student's ID number.

Once logged in, you will need to enter the student's ID number. This number is usually found on the student's transcript or student ID card

Step 3: Click on the "View Profile" button.

After entering the student's ID number, click on the "View Profile" button. This will open the student's profile page.

Step 4: Review the student's information.

The student's profile page will contain a variety of information, including the student's name, contact information, academic history, and grades..

SStep 5: Print or download the student's profile.

If you need to print or download the student's profile, you can do so by clicking on the "Print" or "Download" button.
