Frequently asked questions

Looking for an effortless solution to your entry-level woes? Look no further! Our team has got you covered with the simplest way to tackle those basic problems..

Can Non Universitians / Institutions can create team for free?

We're sorry, but at the moment we only allow university/Institute team members to create an account with us. Don't worry though, if you join a team, you'll be able to access our awesome verified features! For a personal team channel, you can choose one of our starting plans. We appreciate your interest in our platform!

Can We join team or channel from other university?

Absolutely! You are welcome to join the team or channel even if you're from a different university or institution. However, it's ultimately up to the professor to decide if they would like to allow it.

How can I get My account verified ?

After signing up, we'll simply check your college ID and government ID for verification. Within 48 hours, your account will be up and running!.

Who can get free access to the Website?

Currently, only K-12 teachers are eligible for free access, and we kindly ask for proof of identification as a teacher.

Does this website have strong security?

Your data is in safe hands with us! We've made sure to integrate SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) security for an extra layer of protection.
